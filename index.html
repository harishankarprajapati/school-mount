About Mount International School

Mount International School (MIS), an English medium co-educational institute in Ludhiana, Punjab, has quickly established itself as a premier institution under the flagship of the Lala Megh Raj Educational Society. Since its inception in 2015, MIS has been committed to nurturing young learners as future global citizens. The school offers a dynamic and progressive environment, responsive to the evolving world, to foster self-awareness, lifelong learning, and intellectual growth. Following a curriculum affiliated with CBSE, New Delhi, MIS provides a balanced, innovative education rooted in strong pedagogical principles to unlock each child’s unique potential and prepare them to thrive in the 21st century.
