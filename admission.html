Registration for admission to Nursery & K.G. is done for children of age 3 & 4 years as on 15th April.

Pupils coming from a recognized school must produce the School Leaving Certificate. In case of Inter-State transfers, the certificate should be countersigned by the Inspector of schools.

The enrolment form and other necessary papers must be filled in with due care by parents / guardian. The date of birth recorded in the enrolment form cannot be changed later.

A clear calendar month’s notice in writing or one month’s fee in lieu thereof must be given before pupil can be withdrawn.

All dues have to be cleared before a School Leaving Certificate is issued. In respect of withdrawals in May, the fee for June will also be charged.

Students can be asked to leave the school on the following grounds:

(a) Indiscipline,

(b) Unsatisfactory progress in studies

(c) Non-payment of fees
