Inclusivity

Mount International School is deeply committed to fostering an inclusive environment where every child, regardless of their abilities, is given the opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed.

Welcoming All Learners: MIS proudly accepts students with diverse needs, including those requiring additional support, ensuring they feel valued and included in the school community.

Special Educators: Dedicated and trained special educators provide personalised attention and tailored learning strategies to support students with special needs.

Individualised Learning Plans: Customised academic and developmental plans cater to the unique requirements of each student, helping them achieve their full potential.

Holistic Development: Inclusive extracurricular activities ensure that every student participates and thrives beyond academics.

Accessible Infrastructure: Facilities designed to accommodate students with physical and learning challenges make the school environment safe and accessible for all.

At MIS, inclusivity is not just a policy—it’s a commitment to empowering every child to shine in their own unique way.
