Compliances & Regulations

Mount International School strictly adheres to all statutory and regulatory frameworks to ensure a safe, ethical, and high-standard learning environment.

CBSE Affiliation

Fully compliant with the curriculum and guidelines prescribed by CBSE, New Delhi.

Transport Compliance

All school transport vehicles meet safety standards, including GPS tracking, speed governors, and periodic maintenance.

Verified Transport Staff

All drivers and support staff undergo thorough background checks and regular training to prioritise student safety during commutes.

Staff Verification

Thorough background checks and police verification are mandatory for all teaching, non-teaching, and supporting staff.

Health & Safety Protocols

Regular health check-ups, first-aid facilities, and fire safety equipment in accordance with legal standards.

Data Protection

Adherence to privacy norms for safeguarding student and staff data.

Building Safety Certification

All infrastructure is certified as per government safety regulations, ensuring earthquake resistance and fire safety compliance.

Environmental Responsibility

MIS follows eco-friendly practices, including proper waste management and energy-efficient systems.
