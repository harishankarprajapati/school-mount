Basketball

At Mount International School, we are proud of our state-of-the-art basketball court, which serves as a hub for athletic excellence and personal growth. Designed with high-quality flooring, regulation-size hoops, and ample space, it provides an ideal environment for students to hone their skills.

The basketball court is not only used for regular practice and physical education classes but also serves as the venue for our own MIS Championship Trophy. First launched with the basketball competition, this annual event invites schools to participate and showcases our commitment to fostering sportsmanship and competitive spirit.

In addition to hosting internal events, our students actively participate in various external basketball competitions, where they have the opportunity to challenge themselves and represent the school at district, state, and national levels. Through these experiences, students develop teamwork, discipline, and leadership, all of which are integral to their overall development at Mount International School.
