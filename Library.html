Library

A Haven for Learning and Imagination

At Mount International School, we believe that books are a gateway to infinite knowledge and imagination. Our library, a sanctuary for young minds, is home to a meticulously curated collection of over 3,500 books by eminent authors, covering a diverse range of subjects. It serves as a hub for intellectual growth, creativity, and the joy of reading.

The library is thoughtfully designed to foster a love for literature and encourage exploration. Along with books, students have access to newspapers, national and regional periodicals, and reference materials, keeping them informed and expanding their horizons.

To nurture the habit of reading and enhance critical thinking, we organize a variety of activities, including:

Book-writing Competitions: Encouraging students to channel their creativity and pen down their thoughts.

Book Reviews: Developing analytical skills by critically evaluating texts.

Talent Hunts: Identifying and celebrating budding literary talent.
