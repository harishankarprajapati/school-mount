Athletics Facilities

At Mount International School, we have Athletics facilities, where students train for events like Shot Put, Races, Javelin Throw, and Discus Throw. These facilities provide the perfect environment for students to practice and excel.

Our athletes have consistently performed well in district and state competitions, with many qualifying for National-level events.

In Shot Put, students focus on building strength and precision, while in Races, they enhance their speed and endurance. The Javelin Throw and Discus Throw events help students improve their coordination and power.

At Mount International School, our athletics facilities are fully equipped with hurdles, long jump pits, and other essential equipment, ensuring that students have everything they need to train for and excel in various athletics events.
