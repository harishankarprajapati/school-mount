Community Outreach

At Mount International School, we believe in instilling a sense of social responsibility and community service in our students. Through active participation in campaigns and rallies, students raise awareness about important social issues and contribute meaningfully to society.

Under the Community Service Club, our students have organized food distribution drives, participated in clean environment initiatives, and worked towards promoting sustainable practices. They have also engaged in NSS camps, taking the lead in activities such as school cleanliness and community development projects.

These initiatives not only allow students to give back to society but also enhance their teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving skills, shaping them into responsible and compassionate individuals.
