Sports (Outdoor) The concept of overall educational distinction is realized only if the student excels in both academics and sports. We introduce our students to the fascinating world of sports and games with special emphasis on team games like Cricket, Basketball and Badminton. Here, we aim to indoctrinate in them the qualities of sportsmanship and respect for hhierarchy.
Indoor Games As outdoor sports are inseparable part of a student’s growth, so are indoor games. Training in yoga, carom, chess and table tennis enable the child to test herself physically and mentally and be ready for all challenges thrown.
