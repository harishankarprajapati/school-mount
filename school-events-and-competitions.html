school events & competitions

The vibrant calendar of Mount International School is a blend of cultural, academic, and creative events that enrich the student experience. From grand annual functions to thought-provoking debates, innovative exhibitions, and captivating talent shows, every event is designed to nurture talent and foster holistic development.

Annual Functions serve as a platform for students to celebrate creativity and culture through music, dance, drama, and thematic presentations, building confidence and poise.

Debates and Elocution Contests encourage critical thinking, articulate communication, and the ability to present ideas effectively.

Exhibitions, such as science fairs, art displays, and subject-focused showcases, ignite curiosity, innovation, and collaborative learning.

Talent Shows provide a space for students to explore and display their unique abilities, from singing and dancing to instrumental music and creative arts.

Inter-house and inter-school competitions like quizzes, declamations, and sports tournaments promote healthy competition and inspire students to strive for excellence.

These events not only enhance students' confidence but also teach them the values of teamwork, perseverance, and sportsmanship. The calendar is a reflection of the school's commitment to providing every student with opportunities to grow, shine, and develop essential life skills in a dynamic and supportive environment.
