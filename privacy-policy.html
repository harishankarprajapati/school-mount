Mount International School Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Mount International School ("the School") is committed to safeguarding the privacy and security of personal information collected from students, parents, employees, and other stakeholders. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information:

We may collect personal information such as names, addresses, contact details, date of birth, and emergency contact information during the enrollment process.

2.2 Academic Information:

Academic records, grades, and assessment results are collected to facilitate the educational process.

2.3 Health Information:

Health records and information about allergies or medical conditions may be collected to ensure the well-being of students.

3. How We Use Information

3.1 Educational Purposes:

Personal and academic information is used for educational purposes, including enrollment, assessment, and communication with parents.

3.2 Communication:

Contact information may be used for official school communication, newsletters, and event notifications.

3.3 Health and Safety:

Health information is used to ensure the safety and well-being of students and may be shared with relevant staff in case of emergencies.

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Third-Party Service Providers:

We may engage third-party service providers to assist with certain functions, such as cloud storage, communication platforms, or educational tools. These providers adhere to data protection regulations.

4.2 Legal Compliance:

Personal information may be disclosed when required by law or to comply with legal processes.

5. Security Measures

5.1 Data Security:

The School implements security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

5.2 Access Controls:

Access to personal information is restricted to authorized personnel only.

6. Parental Rights

6.1 Access and Correction:

Parents/guardians have the right to access and correct their child's personal information.

6.2 Consent:

The School seeks parental consent for the collection and use of personal information, especially regarding minors.
