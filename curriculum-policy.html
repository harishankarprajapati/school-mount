Curriculum Policy

Mount International School follows the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum, which provides a holistic and student-centred approach to education. The curriculum is aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP) and ensures the overall intellectual, social, and emotional growth of students.

1.Pre-Primary Section (Nursery to UKG)

  • Focuses on fostering foundational skills like language development, early numeracy, and motor skills through play-based learning.
  • Emphasis on creativity, communication, and social-emotional growth.

2. Primary Section (Grades I to V)

  • Our school offers English, Punjabi, Hindi, and French, fostering multilingual proficiency.
  • Lays a strong foundation in core subjects like Mathematics, EVS, Science, and Social Sciences, Computer.
  • Activity-based learning methods are used to develop curiosity and critical thinking.
  • Co-curricular subjects like Abacus, Art, Music, Dance, and Sports are integrated into the curriculum.

3.Middle Section (Grades VI to VIII)

  • Focuses on expanding conceptual knowledge and bridging foundational learning with higher-level understanding.
  • Languages include English, Punjabi, Hindi, and French, fostering multilingual proficiency.
  • Main subjects include Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies along with ICT.
  • Co-curricular subjects like Robotics, Abacus, Art, Music, Dance, and Sports are integrated into the curriculum.
  • Students participate in STEM activities, debates, and projects to foster innovation and teamwork.

4. Senior Section (Grades IX to XII)

  • Languages include English, Punjabi, and Hindi fostering multilingual proficiency.
  • Main subjects include Mathematics, Science (Physics, Chemistry and Biology) and Social Studies (Geography, History, Economics, Civics) along with AI.
  • Co-curricular subjects like Robotics, Abacus, Art, Music, Dance, and Sports are integrated into the curriculum.
  • Our students are prepared for board examinations, career paths and life skills.
  • Students are offered a variety of subjects in Grades XI and XII, ensuring a well-rounded academic experience:

Streams Offered:

  • Medical: English Core, Physics, Chemistry, Biology (with optional subjects like IP, Physical Education, Painting, Psychology, Music etc).
  • Non-Medical: English Core, Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics (with optional subjects like IP, Physical Education, Painting, Psychology, Music etc).
  • Commerce: English Core, Business Studies, Accountancy, Economics (with optional subjects like IP, Physical Education, Painting, Psychology, Music etc).
  • Humanities: English Core, Geography, Punjabi, History, Political Science, Sociology (with optional subjects like IP, Physical Education, Painting, Psychology, Music etc).

Skill-Based Subjects:

In addition to core subjects, we offer CBSE-approved skill-based subjects at every level to enhance employability and practical knowledge. These include Artificial Intelligence, Financial Literacy, and Communication Skills.
