Fee Policy

Important Instructions Regarding Fee Payment

Fee Structure For 2024-25

Payment should be made BY CHEQUE/ DD/ PAY ORDER ONLY in favour of Mount International School, Ludhiana

Do write Student’s Name, Class, Section, Father’s Name and Phone No. at the back of the Cheque

Give Separate Cheque for each child

Don’t give post-dated Cheques

Give only CTS Cheques

Receipt of Cheque will be subject to the clearance by the Bank

Non-Receipt of fee circular will not be considered as a reason for non-payment of fees in time.

Parents can also pay the fee for full year in one go.

Follow single queue system while waiting for your turn at the fee window.

No student will be permitted to appear in the examination without fee clearance

No refund will be made, once the receipt is issued.

If any child fails to pay the fees for two consecutive terms, his/her name will be struck off and the child will be declared ineligible to continue his/her studies. He/she can be re-admitted after paying the full admission fee, provided there is a vacant seat.

Financial Support

Merit-Based Concessions - On the basis of percentage of marks secured in Board Examination, fee concession is provided to the students. Special Fee Concession is granted to the students who bring glory to the school in various sports and co-scholastic activities like Music, Dance, Drama, Literary Activities and Fine Arts.

With regard to the timely payment of fees, it has been observed in the past years that many parents fail to clear their dues as a result of which their wards remain in the category of Fee Defaulters till the end of the session. It is expected from all the parents to be very particular in depositing the Fee Installments regularly and on time.

Bus route - MIS follows and maintains a high standard of transportation system as per Safe School Vahan Scheme of the Govt. to ensure the safety and security of the students. Kindly note the following:

All the buses are owned and operated by the school. The buses are A.C. and equipped with GPS tracking and CCTV.

Every bus has a female and male conductor to take care and ensure the safety of the children.

No child will be dropped off at any other spot other than the designated stop of the child.

Parent/Guardian should be available at the stop to receive the child at his/her stop. In case no one is present, the child will be brought back to school.

Any change in the pick up or drop point for the child has to be conveyed in writing to the school at least 2 days before.
