Proctorial Board

At Mount International School, we take pride in our Proctorial Board, comprising a Junior and Senior Student Council, elected through a structured process of voting campaigns and a formal polling day. The elected members are officially inducted during a grand Investiture Ceremony, where they take an oath to uphold their duties with dedication and integrity.

At Mount International School, we take pride in our Proctorial Board, comprising a Junior and Senior Student Council, elected through a structured process of voting campaigns and a formal polling day. The elected members are officially inducted during a grand Investiture Ceremony, where they take an oath to uphold their duties with dedication and integrity.

The council works diligently to contribute to the success of school events and functions while striving for the overall betterment of the school environment.

Serving on the Proctorial Board provides students with a valuable platform to develop and showcase their leadership skills, teamwork, and organizational abilities, empowering them to grow into confident and responsible individuals.
